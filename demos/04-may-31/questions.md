## Aggregate function

Here are 4 practice queries (without answers) involving aggregate functions using the world and sakila databases. No GROUP BY or HAVING are required, but queries build on fundamental aggregate use:

---

### Medium

1. **world**  
   Find the total population of all countries in the world.

2. **sakila**  
   What is the average length of all films in the film table?

---

### Complex

3. **world**

4. **sakila**  
   What is the sum of all replacement_cost values for films that have a rental duration of more than 5 days?
